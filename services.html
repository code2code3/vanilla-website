Painting and Decorating

High quality professional residential and commercial decorating services. We can help you from start to finish consulting with you to find, and organising just the right paint and finish. We have an extensive portfolio of all kinds of interior and exterior projects, and have the expertise to give you that professional finish that lasts. Whether it’s a new build, a victorian pile or a medieval castle you need decorating, we know how to help, and are proficient in both paint and paper.

Floorboard Restoration

Does your wooden floor need a new finish? We can help, with a comprehensive floorboard restoration service. From sanding-back, to sealing, and from draft proofing, to a new rich varnish, we can do it all. If you are thinking your boards are beyond help, book a consultation and we can come to the rescue.

Design

We know how important it is to love the spaces you are in, and we know how hard it is to get them looking just perfect. This is why as well as all aspects of decorating, we also offer a bespoke design consultation service. For those who need an extra hand with making decorative decisions, our colour and design experts will consult with you and help put together a cohesive palette which just ‘works’ for your space. Whatever your desired look, we can help you achieve it, and help you build a space that you love. Please see our gallery for some of our recent work.
