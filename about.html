About

Tressell and Vine is the working name of a painting and decorating service provided by tradespeople of Hastings. The name comes from The Ragged Trousered Philanthropists by Robert Tressell - a novel based on the author’s time living in the town.

Whether residential or commercial properties, new-build or Victorian terrace, we have decades of combined experience in painting and decorating offering a complete service from design concept through to furnishing.

We pride ourselves on our friendly demure and professional attitude.
